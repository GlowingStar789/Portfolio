Amazing Movies Based On My Experience

3 Of The Most Memorable Movies

The Super Mario Bros Movie

While the storyline is different in comparison to the video game that it is based on, it is still a remarkable representation of the iconic Mario Nintendo franchise
Overall a very thrilling CGI action movie

The FNAF Movie

A movie based on the horror game of the same name. While it is a horror movie, it isn't that scary. At least just a few scares.
The way it interpreted the storyline of the events throughout the first FNAF game and a bit of the third game was spot on

The Barbie Movie

This movie is a good example of don't judge a book by it's cover. While its based on a doll toy children play with, the movie has a really deep meaning.
It really stands out from other barbie moviess, its not a typical one
